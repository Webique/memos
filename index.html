OUR CRAFT

At Memos, our chef's expertise in crafting Neapolitan pizza is distinguished by precision and craftsmanship.

The story begins with the pizza dough, which the chef handles with exceptional care. Through a slow fermentation process lasting 48 hours, the chef allows the dough to transform into a flavorful masterpiece. This process ensures the perfect texture—light, crispy, and firm all at once.

The skill goes beyond the meticulous mixing of ingredients, extending to the chef’s careful consideration of each moment while distributing the ingredients evenly across the pizza's surface. Time is allowed to work its magic on the dough, resulting in a taste that satisfies the senses and delights the palate.

Drawing inspiration from the authentic Neapolitan tradition, our chef ensures unrivaled quality and taste in every slice.
